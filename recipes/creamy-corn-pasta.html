Creamy Corn Pasta

Description

This corn pasta is deliciously creamy...without any cream! Blended corn creates the sauce's luscious texture. Fresh basil takes it over the top.

Preparation Time: 15 mins

Cook Time: 10 mins

Total Time: 25 mins

Ingredients

  • 3 cups of fresh corn kernels
  • medium shells pasta
  • shallots and garlic
  • extra-virgin olive oil
  • fresh lemon juice
  • pecorino cheese
  • red pepper flakes
  • salt and pepper
  • fresh basil leaves
