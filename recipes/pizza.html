Pepperoni Pizza

Description

Pepperoni pizza is a popular and well-loved pizza topping that features sliced pepperoni, a type of spicy salami, on top of a pizza crust, with tomato sauce and melted cheese.

Preparation Time: 10 mins

Cook Time: 15 mins

Total Time: 25 mins

Ingredients

  • 16 ounces pizza dough
  • 1/2 cup pizza sauce
  • 18 to 20 slices pepperoni
  • 12 ounces mozzarella cheese, grated
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon fresh oregano, optional
  • Flour for rolling and shaping dough

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 500*F
  2. Make the sauce
  3. Roll out the dough
  4. Add the toppings
  5. Cook the pizza
  6. Slice and serve
