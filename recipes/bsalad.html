Burger Salad

Description

This is a hamburger in salad form with well-seasoned burger patties, crisp iceberg lettuce, and all of your favorite burger toppings.

Ingredients

For the dressing/sauce - 1 cup

  • 1/2 cup mayonnaise
  • 1/4 cup ketchup
  • 2 tablespoons yellow mustard
  • 2 tablespoons dill pickle relish
  • 1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper

For the salads:

  • 8 cups chopped iceberg lettuce
  • 1/2 medium red onion sliced thinly
  • 1 beefsteak
  • 24 dill pickle chips
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese

Steps

  1. Shape the burgers
  2. Cook the burgers
  3. While the burgers are cooking, make the dressing
  4. Assemble and serve the salads
